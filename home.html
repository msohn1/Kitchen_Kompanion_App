Suggested Recipes

Recipe 1: Breakfast Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 egg
  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 1 slice of cheese

Steps

  1. Cook bacon until crispy and set aside.
  2. Fry an egg to your liking.
  3. Toast the bread slices.
  4. Assemble sandwich with egg, bacon, and cheese.

Recipe 2: Garlic Chicken Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce

Steps

  1. Dice chicken and sauté with minced garlic.
  2. Add soy sauce and cook until browned.
  3. Serve over a bowl of rice.

Recipe 3: Mozzarella Toast

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tomato
  • 2 slices mozzarella
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Brush bread with olive oil and toast lightly.
  2. Add tomato and mozzarella slices on top.
  3. Toast again until cheese melts.
